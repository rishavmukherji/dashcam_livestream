  8. BENEFITS


    • Real-Time Community-Inclusive Police Surveillance
    • Increased Community Reactivity
    • Increased Police Accountability and Transparency
    • Combats Racial Profiling and Excessive Use of Force
    • Evidenciary and Training Purposes

  9. VULNERABILITIES


    1. Access to Live Data by Mailicious Actors
    2. Access to Stored Data by Mailicious Actors
    3. Police Tampering
    4. Recording on Private Property
    5. Limited Camera Angle
    6. Unreliable Internet Connectivity
    7. High Cost of Implementation

  10. SOLUTION

    (Vulnerabilities > Benefits)


    1. Traditional Dashcams > Live Dashcams
    2. Online Accessibility of Videos to Public
